About Me

Kiran, a product designer at Quil (an AI recruiting software company in Canada), excels in crafting user-centric solutions to elevate digital experiences. Collaborating closely with her team, she dives into user needs, ideates, and delivers design solutions that not only enhance user satisfaction and loyalty but also elevate the company's NPS and revenue. Currently, she is focusing on the aspects that set Quil apart from its competitors and ensures customer retention through innovative designs. Apart from her design work, Kiran is also a mentor who teaches UX Design to underprivileged youth through the Institute of Emerging Careers (IEC). She mentors young people to help them secure jobs in the tech industry, which is her way of making a positive impact in her home country, Pakistan.
